What is Vinna?
 Vinna is a digital advertising platform where Associated Businesses pay their Customers for buying and recommending Products and Services. Our purpose is to create an ever expanding community where everyone receives a profit for what they contribute. Together we can change "Buy and Sale" for "Win and Win".
Buy and Win
 After signing up as a Member, you can look for Local Business on our Directory. Every time you buy in those Businesses you receive a 2% instant refund. Just show your code to the cashier before paying to register the sale.
Share and Grow
 You can share Vinna with friends and family and invite them to join as Members. Each time they buy in our Business Partners you will receive 4% commission for each sale. There are no limits in Vinna, the more people you connect, the more income you generate.
Help and Change
 As a Member, if you own a business, now you can increase your sales through personal recommendations from your clients, while you help them make money and live a better life. All this, with 100% guarantee in results and zero risk, since in Vinna, Businesses pay for advertising only after each sale.
